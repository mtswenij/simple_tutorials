About Ajira

What is Ajira?

Ajira is a Micro-employment Mobile Application / Web Service for Developing Countries. It allows individuals whom are seeking employment to connect with other people who need a service or task accomplished.

What is Appropriate Technology?

Appropriate technology is technology that takes special consideration of the context - environmental, ethical, economic, and social - of its users. With these goals in mind, Appropriate technology typically relies more on local resources, is easier to maintain, has a lower overall cost and impact on the environment.

How does Ajira take advantage of Appropriate Technology?

In many developing countries around the world technology use is currently on the rise. Although, in some areas there still are not many computers a with Internet access, there are people that own or have access to cell a phone. We plan to develop an SMS interface that will consume our service. This interface will allow any person with text messaging capabilities to take advantage of Ajira.
